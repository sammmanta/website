Juan Paulo García is a specialist and researcher in the development of skills and abilities aimed at integrating soccer and life. He is the founder and Head coach of Club Jugar FL, President of the Bogotá Soccer Coaches Association and lecturer. He has dedicated his life to teaching soccer, promoting sports competition between organizations and clubs in Colombia, Aruba, and Argentina. His work stands out for his vision and training philosophy, leaving a lasting impact and providing players with the tools and experiences necessary to compete and grow as soccer players and human beings.
